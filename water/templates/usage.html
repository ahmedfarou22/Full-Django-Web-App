Usage

This page includes a step by step guide to find the water usage



Step One

  • Using the navigation bar above click on login or create an account if you do not have one.

Step Two

  • Using the navigation bar above click on Map. This will take you to the search page

Step Three

  • Click on the map to get the cordinates of the land, and From the dropdown list choose the crop type

Step Four

  • Click on the sumbit button and you will be redirected to the results page where you can view the results
