WHY ARE WE HERE?

Our planet faces many obstacles in a rapidly evolving world that produces a great amount of waste. These obstacles threaten our planet as well as all its inhibitants, and one of these major obstacles is the overconsumpution of water.

OUR SOLUTION

It is reported that 70% of the consumable water is directed towards the agricultural sector, and 40% of that value is wasted during the process. Decreasing the surplus used would greatly help humans conserve water that might be difficult to come by in areas of the world that suffer from droughts.

We aim to decrease the surplus by creating an app that will be used to determine how much surplus water is being used on a plot of land, and this will help farmers across the world to manage their water usage effectively
